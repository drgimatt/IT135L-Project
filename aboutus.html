VOLUNTEERS!

Kids of Bataan is a charity organization that aims to make the world a happier place for children. Powered by youth volunteers, this organization sees itself as a catalyst of joy and inspiration for the next generation of Bataeños.

4 CORE VALUES

LEADERSHIP

Kids of Bataan values the involvement of youth volunteers dedicated to leading by example and inspiring the next generation to take charge. We believe that effective leadership is the driving force behind positive change, progress, and the betterment of the community.

CHARACTER

Kids of Bataan upholds the value of character rooted in our commitment to fostering a culture of respect, kinndness, responsibility, and integrity both among our youth volunteers and beneficiaries, nurturing a community where character is a guiding light for a safer world for children.

COMMUNITY

Kids of Bataan places a strong emphasis on inclusivity and enriching a sense of togetherness, where every volunteer actively participates in improving the community and is dedicated to the organization's unwavering commitment to ensure that no Bataeño child gets left behind.

INSPIRATION

Kids of Bataan commits to being a constant source of inspiration for the children we serve and our peers. We believe that inspiration goes beyond just words; it's about motivating the next generation to dream big, believe in themselves, and take action to make those dreams a reality.
